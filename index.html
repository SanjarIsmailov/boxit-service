ABOUT BOXIT

Removals built around the way people actually move

BOXIT started in London in 2016 with a single van and a simple idea: moving house shouldn't feel like a second job. Ten years on, our crews cover England, Scotland and Wales, handling homes, flats and offices with the same care we'd want for our own belongings.

We're not the biggest removals firm in the country, and we like it that way. Every crew is trained in-house, every van is checked before it leaves the depot, and every move gets a named coordinator from quote to final box.

FAQ

Questions we're asked before moving day

Do you provide packing materials?

Yes. We supply boxes, tape, bubble wrap and wardrobe cartons, either as part of a full-service move or for you to collect and pack yourself in advance.

Is my furniture insured during the move?

Every booking includes goods-in-transit and public liability insurance as standard, at no extra cost. Full policy details are confirmed at your free survey.

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend two to four weeks' notice for weekday moves, and four to six weeks for weekends or the end of the month, when demand is highest. Man & van bookings can often be arranged with a few days' notice.

Do you move outside London?

Yes. BOXIT operates across England, Scotland and Wales, with regular routes between London, Manchester, Birmingham, Bristol and Edinburgh.

Can you dismantle and reassemble furniture?

Our crews carry the tools to dismantle and reassemble wardrobes, beds and flat-pack furniture at both ends of the move, included in a full-service booking.

Do you offer storage if my new home isn't ready?

Yes, we offer secure short and long-term storage, so your move can go ahead even if there's a gap between properties.
